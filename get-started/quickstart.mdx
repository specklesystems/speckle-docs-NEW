<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Speckle account">
    Visit [speckle.systems](https://app.speckle.systems/) and create an account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download and Install">
    After creating your account, visit [speckle.systems/connectors](https://app.speckle.systems/connectors) and download the Connector for your software (e.g., Rhino, AutoCAD, SketchUp, etc.).  
    
    Run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions.
    <Warning>Please ensure that the target software is closed during the installation process.</Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Speckle">
    After creating your account and installing the Connector for your application, launch the software.  

    Look for the **Speckle tab** or **menu option**, then click the **Speckle icon** to open the Connector window.

    If you're using a command-based application (like Rhino), simply type `Speckle` into the command line to launch the Connector.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign in (if needed)">
    If this is your first time, you'll likely be prompted to **Sign In** and connect your Speckle account.  
  </Step>

  <Step title="Publish to Speckle">
    With the Speckle window open, click **Publish**.<br />

    Select the destination for your model:
      - **Workspace** – Pick the workspace where your project belongs  
      - **Project** – Choose an existing project or click the **+** to create a new one  
      - **Model** – Select or create a model to hold your data    

    Finally, click **Publish** to send your model to Speckle.

    With the Speckle window open, click **Load**.<br />
    Select your **Workspace**, then choose the **Project** and **Model** you want to load.<br />
    Choose the **version** you want to receive.
