  <Accordion title="How to create an Account?">
    To start using Speckle, you’ll need to create a free account. Follow these steps:

    1. Go to [app.speckle.systems](https://app.speckle.systems) and register using:
      - Your email and a secure password, **or** your  Google account

    2. Once you’ve signed up, you’ll receive a verification email.  
      Click the link in the email to verify your account.

    3. After verifying, you can log in and start exploring the Speckle web app.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How to install a Speckle Connector?">

    Follow these steps to install a Connector:

    1. Go to the [Connectors page](https://app.speckle.systems/connectors).

    2. Find the Connector for the software you use (e.g., Revit, Rhino, Blender).

    3. Click **Download** next to the Connector you want.

    4. Run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How to create a Project?">

    Before publishing or receiving models, you'll need a project to organize your data. Here's how to set one up:

    1. Projects live inside **Workspaces**. Choose an existing one, or click **+ New Workspace** if you need to create a new one for your team.

    2. Inside your selected workspace, click the **New Project** button. Give your project a **name** and click **Create** to finish setting up your project.

    3. You can invite teammates to your project by adding them as **Editors** or **Viewer** under the Collaborators tab in you project.
    <Note>
    Learn more about [Projects here.](http://localhost:3000/webApp/projects)
    </Note>

  <Accordion title="How to publish a Model with a Speckle Connector?">
      <Steps>
      <Step title="Open your project">
      Launch the software where you installed the Connector (e.g., Revit, Rhino, or Grasshopper), and open the project you want to publish.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Open the Speckle Connector">
      Look for the **Speckle tab** and **Speckle button** in your software, then click to open the Connector window.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Sign in (if needed)">
      If you haven’t signed in yet, click **Sign In** and connect your Speckle account.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Click Publish">
      In the Connector window, click the **Publish** button to begin the process.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Choose where to send your model?">
      Select the destination for your model:
      - **Workspace** – Pick the workspace where your project belongs  
      - **Project** – Choose an existing project or click the **+** to create a new one  
      - **Model** – Select or create a model to hold your data
      </Step>

      <Step title="Select and publish">
      Choose the objects you want to send, then click **Publish** to upload your model to Speckle.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How to Load a Model with a Speckle Connector?">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open your project">
      Launch your software (e.g., Revit, Rhino, Grasshopper) and open the file where you want to load the model.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Open the Speckle Connector">
      Look for the **Speckle tab** and **Speckle Button** inside your software, then click to open the Speckle Connector.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Sign in (if needed)">
      If you haven’t signed in yet, click **Sign In** and connect your Speckle account.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Click Load">
      In the Connector window, click the **Load** button to begin.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Choose what to load">
      Select your **Workspace**, then choose the **Project**, **Model**, and the **version** you want to receive.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Load the model">
      Click **Load** to bring the selected model into your file.  
      Depending on the Connector, the data will appear as reference geometry or native objects.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
