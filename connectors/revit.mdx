## Getting Started

Welcome! This guide will walk you through how to **publish** and **load** your Revit models using the **Speckle Connector for Revit**.

    Select your **Workspace**, then choose an existing **Project** and **Model**, or create new ones by clicking the **+** icon. Choose the objects you want to publish using one of the three methods: [**Selection**, **View Selection**, or **Category Selection**](/connectors/revit#publish-options).<br />
    
    Finally, click **Publish** to send your model to Speckle.
