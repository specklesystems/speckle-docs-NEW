Welcome! This guide will walk you through how to **publish** your Navisworks models using **Navisworks connector**.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Step 1: Open Navisworks Connector">
    <Note>
      Before you begin, make sure you have a Speckle account and [installed
      Navisworks Connector](http://localhost:3000/connectors/installation).
    </Note>
    <Steps>
      <Step
        title="In Navisworks, select the Speckle tab in the ribbon."
        description="Open the Speckle connector in Navisworks."
      ></Step>
      <Step title="Select Speckle to open the Navisworks connector."></Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Step 2: Publish to Speckle">
    <Note>Navisworks connector is publish only.</Note>
    <Steps>
      <Step
        title="In the Speckle connector, select Publish."
        description="Choose a project and model to publish your Navisworks elements."
      ></Step>
      <Step
        title="Select a project and model."
        description="You can either select an existing project/model or create a new one."
      ></Step>
      <Step
        title="Select the elements you want to publish in your Navisworks viewport."
        description="You can select multiple elements at once."
      ></Step>
      <Step title="Click Publish to upload your model to Speckle."></Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
