<Info>Speckle currently supports **Blender 4.2, 4.3, 4.4, and 4.5**. The Blender connector works on **Windows** only.</Info>

## Getting started

Welcome! This guide will walk you through how to **publish** your Blender models and **load** other models using the **Blender connector**.

import BeforeYouBegin from "/snippets/before-you-begin.mdx";

<BeforeYouBegin app="Blender" />

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Blender connector">
    1. Press **N** from your keyboard to open the side toolbar.
    2. Select **Speckle** to open the Blender connector.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Publish to Speckle">
    1. In the Speckle connector, select **Publish**.
    2. Choose a [project]().
    3. Choose a [model]().
    4. In your Blender viewport, select the elements you want to publish.
    5. Select **Publish**.
    
    Your model will be uploaded to Speckle and becomes available with the rest of your team.
  </Step>


    <Step title="Load from Speckle">
      1. In the Speckle connector, select **Load**.
    2. Select the [project]().
    3. Select the [model]().
    4. Choose a [version]():
          - Latest version is loaded by default.
          - To load a specific version, select it from the UI.
      5. Select **Load**.

      Selected model will be loaded in Blender.
    </Step>

</Steps>

<Tip> Check the video and steps in the [first steps](/get-started/quickstart.mdx) section to get started even faster!</Tip>
