    <Accordion title="Are licenses floating?">
        Seats inside Speckle are named, meaning `1 Editor seat = 1 User`, but admins can swap users between Editor and Viewer (free) seats at any time.
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="What's the billing frequency?">
        Frequency is **monthly** or **annual** billing, it can be selected during checkout. For invoice payments only annual billing is available
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Can I get a receipt or invoice for my card payment?">
        Yes, from the billing page open the Stripe portal. From there you will be able to download invoices and receipts and set up automatic emails.
    </Accordion>
     <Accordion title="Do you offer a free Trial?">
